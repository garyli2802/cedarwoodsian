How it works

Go to the order page

You can simply input your basic information and your shopping list and click the "Submit order" button.

Place an order

After submitted the order list, you will receive a confiramtion email. If your order placed before 12:00pm, you will receive your order on the same day. If your order placed after 12:00pm, you will receive your order on the following day.

Grocery delivery and pay

We will deliver the grocery straight to your door step between 3pm to 6pm. You can pay the total price amount with service fee by cash or Interac (recommended).

Why Cedarwoods-ian

With Cedarwoods-ian, you no longer need to spend time and energy to go grocery store to buy what you need. You can order your groceries online and wait for the delivery straight to your doorstep.
